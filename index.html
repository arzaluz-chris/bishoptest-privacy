Privacy Policy

Introduction

Thank you for using BishopTest. This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use our application.

BishopTest is a medical application designed to assist healthcare professionals in performing Bishop Score evaluations for obstetric assessments. We understand the sensitive nature of medical data and are committed to protecting your privacy.

Information We Collect

BishopTest collects and processes the following information:

  • Patient Information: Names, ages, and previous delivery history that you enter into the application.
  • Medical Data: Bishop Score parameters and evaluation results.
  • User Preferences: Settings such as display preferences and professional information that you choose to provide.

All of this information is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to external servers or third parties.

How We Use Your Information

The information collected through BishopTest is used exclusively for:

  • Providing the core functionality of the application (Bishop Score calculations and evaluations)
  • Maintaining a local history of evaluations for your reference
  • Improving the user experience based on your preferences

Your data is not used for marketing, advertising, or any other commercial purposes.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

BishopTest does not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third parties. Your data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers or to any third parties for any purpose.

We do not have access to the data you enter into the application, and we do not collect any usage statistics or analytics.

Data Security

We are committed to ensuring the security of your information. Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of this information is subject to the security measures you have implemented on your device (such as device passcodes, Touch ID, or Face ID).

We recommend that you:

  • Keep your device's operating system and the BishopTest app updated to the latest version
  • Use strong device passcodes or biometric protection
  • Be cautious about allowing others to access your device

Your Rights

You have full control over your data within BishopTest. You can:

  • Access all patient data stored in the app
  • Delete individual records or all data from the app
  • Export or share evaluation results as needed for medical purposes

Since we do not collect or store your data on our servers, there is no need to request access, correction, or deletion of data from us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
